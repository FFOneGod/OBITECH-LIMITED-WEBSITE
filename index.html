ABOUT US

Get to
know Us

ObiTech Nigeria Limited is an Industrial Engineering Services, Mechanical Pipeline and General Contracting firm incorporated in Nigeria with registration number RC. No364529. The company is made up of registered Engineers and Technicians who have handled projects loally and internally since 1999.

We are a 2nd-generation Industrial Mechanical Services company with Engineers who have a wealth of experience working in and around the Industrial sector servicing Commercial and Industrial scale clientele.

Obi Tech Nigeria Limited is focused on serving the Industrial sector and caters to a variety of Industries like Food & Beverage, Chemical Processing Plants, Cement Plants, Power Plants, Aluminum Foundries, Paper mills, Water plants, Medical Waste, and Wastewater facilities to name a few.

Essentially we work in all Heavy Industries with Heavy Fabrication and Welding being our top requests.
